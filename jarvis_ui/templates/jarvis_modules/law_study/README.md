# Law Study

Purpose:
Legal Education, Legal Research & Law Learning System for JARVIS.

Status:
Development

Version:
1.0

Owner:
JARVIS Project

Phase:
3

Description:
Law Study is the legal education and legal knowledge platform of JARVIS. It is designed to help law students, advocates, researchers, NGOs and citizens study Indian laws including BNS, BNSS, Evidence Act, Constitution, Supreme Court judgments and legal procedures.

The system will eventually become a complete offline legal learning platform with AI-powered explanations, legal search, quizzes, legal memory and judgment analysis.

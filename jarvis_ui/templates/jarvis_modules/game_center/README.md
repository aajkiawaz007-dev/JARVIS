Description:
Game Center is the gaming and entertainment module of JARVIS. It is designed to provide offline games, AI opponents, simulations, educational games, multiplayer systems, virtual environments and future VR/AR experiences.

The system will eventually become a complete entertainment ecosystem capable of running strategy games, sports games, educational games, simulations, AI-generated worlds and immersive experiences.
